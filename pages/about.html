Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized research laboratory that pioneers transformative innovations in computer vision and artificial intelligence, driving positive societal impact through cutting-edge technology and interdisciplinary collaboration.

We envision a future where intelligent systems seamlessly integrate with human society, enhancing quality of life, advancing scientific discovery, and solving complex global challenges through the power of visual intelligence.

Our Mission

To advance the frontiers of computer vision, machine learning, and robotics through excellence in research, education, and innovation. We are committed to:

  • Conducting world-class fundamental and applied research
  • Training the next generation of AI researchers and engineers
  • Fostering industry partnerships for real-world impact
  • Promoting ethical and responsible AI development
  • Contributing to the global research community

Research Focus Areas

Our multidisciplinary research spans several key areas of AI and computer vision

Computer Vision & Image Processing

Advanced algorithms for object detection, segmentation, tracking, and scene understanding in complex environments.

Deep Learning & Neural Networks

Novel architectures, optimization techniques, and transfer learning methods for visual recognition tasks.

Autonomous Systems & Robotics

Intelligent navigation, manipulation, and human-robot interaction for service and industrial applications.

Medical Image Analysis

AI-powered diagnostic tools, disease detection, and precision medicine applications.

Video Understanding & Action Recognition

Temporal modeling, activity recognition, and video analytics for surveillance and content analysis.

3D Vision & Reconstruction

Depth estimation, 3D modeling, SLAM, and augmented reality applications.
